You are the commander of a United Earth Space Navy (UESN) cruiser.


While escorting a merchant convoy, you came under attack by unidentified organic lifeforms emerging from transpatial portals.

Although you and your crew fought valiantly, your UESN vessel ended up being drawn into one of the tears in spatial reality.
You, your crew, and your ship now find yourselves alone in unidentified space.
After attempting to contact command, you find your communications systems are out of range.
Once brief repairs are completed, you consult with your officers and decide to navigate back to Earth Command in order to report events and receive new orders.
